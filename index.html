We accept refund, replacement or exchange requests under the conditions:

  • Item arrives damaged

  • Incorrect product shipped/recieved

To be eligible, you must:

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery

  • Provide your order number

  • Include clear photo evidence of issue

  • Unworn items in original condition can be exchanged or returned


Is N✪VAE Fast-Fashion?

No, we try our hardest to stay slow, intentional, and circular. Every piece we created is made to be kept, loved, and eventually remade.


Rather than discarding products, Novae's team looks for ways to extend its life and reduce waste — from using non-toxic dyes, water-based inks, plant-based inks, and water-based coatings, to choosing recyclable, shrink-wrap-free sleeves and shipping boxes made with up to 93% recycled material.


We also prioritize organic and recycled fibers wherever possible across our merchandise, including 100% recycled cotton, and the water used in our production process is recycled and treated for circularity and safe discharge.


All our paper packaging is FSC® certified recycled paper, and we are proudly PETA Cruelty-Free!


Additionally, "Look of the Month" is designed to use our less popular products and encourage you to become more resourceful.❤︎


Is N✪VAE's Products Suitable for Sensitive skin, Eczema, or allergies?

Yes, we design with these conditions in mind.


Our jewellery is nickel-free and lead-free, along with only using hypoallergenic components across all products — clothing and more! Every material is chosen to minimize irritation, so you can wear our pieces with confidence.


If you have a specific allergy or concern, feel free to reach out, we're happy to share the full material breakdown for any item.
